Hi, I'm Jon.

I explore, analyze, and build.

I explore and seek knowledge

I recently finished my PhD in Chemical & Biological Engineering at Northwestern University under the guidance of Keith Tyo and Linda Broadbelt. You can find my publications here.

I love to think about how we can make processes more sustainable, especially by harnessing the power of biology. I also enjoy the process of learning, whether that be learning about a metabolic pathway or a software engineering best practice.

I am currently combining these two passions as a Scientist at LanzaTech on the AI & Modeling team.

I analyze and interpret data

I love delving into data and finding hidden patterns. I have experience with machine learning, developing mathematical models, as well as performing both frequentist and Bayesian statistical analyses. The python data science stack is my bread and butter, and I enjoy applying these skills in close collaboration with experimentalists.

I build useful software tools

I enjoy the development process, and I try to build tools that are useful for others. I have built web APIs and small websites using flask, and I built this site using Jekyll. During my PhD, I built MINE 2.0 a research tool for discovering new biochemical compounds (see publication).
